WITH region_summary AS (
    SELECT
        Region,
        ROUND(SUM(Sales), 2)                    AS revenue,
        ROUND(SUM(Profit), 2)                   AS profit,
        ROUND(SUM(Profit)/SUM(Sales)*100, 2)    AS margin_pct
    FROM superstore
    GROUP BY Region
)
SELECT
    Region,
    revenue,
    profit,
    margin_pct,
    RANK() OVER (ORDER BY profit DESC)            AS profit_rank,
    ROUND(profit * 100.0 / SUM(profit) OVER(), 1) AS profit_share_pct
FROM region_summary
ORDER BY profit_rank;


Finding 4: West region is the profit leader (37.9% of total profit,
14.94% margin). Central is underperforming — higher revenue than 
South but lower profit and nearly half the margin of West (7.92% 
vs 14.94%). Central region needs pricing and discount review.
